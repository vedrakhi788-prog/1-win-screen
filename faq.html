Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Trompo Kids Game.

What ages are suitable to attend?

The venue offers activities and services for all ages; some areas and programs may have age restrictions or require adult supervision for minors.

How do I buy tickets?

Tickets can be purchased online or at the box office, subject to availability. We offer group and school packages with special rates.

Are there options for groups or school visits?

Yes. We offer school programs and guided group visits. Contact our team to coordinate dates and educational activities.

Is the venue accessible?

We provide adapted access and staff support for visitors with special needs. Please notify us in advance to arrange specific assistance.

What safety measures are in place?

We enforce strict safety protocols, access control, animal welfare procedures, and trained staff for emergencies and first aid.
