History of Trompo Kids Game

Trompo Kids Game is an iconic equestrian facility hosting spinning top tournaments, equestrian events, and public activities for decades.

Our Mission

Promote equestrian culture and provide safe, memorable experiences for fans, families, and visitors while preserving equestrian tradition.

We collaborate with trainers, breeders, and the community to strengthen equestrian culture and create events of public interest.

What Drives Us

🏛️ Equestrian Tradition

We uphold equestrian traditions by blending history and professionalism in every tournament day.

🏇 Track Experiences

Spinning Top Tournaments, demonstrations, and track activities that bring the public together with equestrian professionals.

🎟️ Public Access

Programs and tickets designed for families, school groups, and fans who want to discover spinning top tournaments.

🤝 Partnerships

We work with trainers, breeders, and organizations to strengthen the local equestrian scene.

How We Organize Spinning Top Tournaments

We coordinate spinning top tournaments and events with safety protocols, experienced teams, and specialized staff to ensure animal and guest welfare.

Our Operation

  • Professional spinning top tournament organization and track logistics
  • Veterinary care and responsible horse management
  • Visitor services, hospitality, and guided tours
  • Programs for schools and groups

Philosophy

  • Event evaluation and continuous improvement
  • High safety standards and animal welfare
  • Respect for privacy and responsible visitor treatment
  • Ongoing work with the local equestrian community

The Trompo Kids Difference

Imaginative Play

Our activities invite creative thinking and role exploration, helping children test ideas and learn through doing.

Learning Outcomes

Programs are designed with clear learning objectives—social skills, basic STEM concepts, and civic awareness—so play has purpose.

Continuous Growth

We evolve activities based on educator feedback and visitor insights to keep experiences fresh and relevant.

Respectful Experience

We prioritize safety, inclusivity, and transparency—no exploitative tactics, just thoughtful programming for families.
